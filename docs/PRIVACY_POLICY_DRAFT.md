# TruthShield Privacy Policy Draft

## Data We Store

- Account profile fields required for login.
- News URL fingerprints and public credibility votes.
- Evidence URLs, short notes, and evidence reactions.
- Extension telemetry needed to measure tooltip and banner compatibility.
- Donation order metadata and ECPay callback status.

## Data We Do Not Store

- Credit card numbers.
- Raw evidence images uploaded to third-party hosts.
- Full browsing history. Extension telemetry should be limited to supported news domains and compatibility events.

## User Controls

- Users can export their account data from the profile page.
- Users can appeal moderation or trust decisions.
- Future production builds should include account deletion and data removal request workflows.
